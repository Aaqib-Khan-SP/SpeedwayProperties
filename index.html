About Us




In today's dynamic and ever-evolving real estate landscape, Speedway Properties emerges as a steadfast leader, navigating the industry's dramatic transformations with resilience and innovation. For nearly two decades, we have proudly established ourselves as a prominent player in the global market, poised to soar to even greater heights in the coming years.

The restructuring of the global economy has ushered in a new era of opportunities and challenges, prompting Speedway Properties to chart a strategic course towards expansion across the UAE and into the wider Gulf sector. This forward-thinking approach has enabled us to witness and actively participate in one of the most significant transformations in the region's real estate sector. Despite the myriad challenges we have faced, our unwavering commitment to excellence has propelled us forward, driving remarkable progress and growth.

At Speedway Properties, we possess an intimate understanding of both local nuances and international dynamics, engaging with the industry daily and meticulously monitoring trends across every facet of the real estate value chain. Our esteemed track record includes collaborations with some of the most reputable names in the UAE market, where we have played a pivotal role in shaping and advancing the industry's development.

Looking ahead, Speedway Properties aspires to ascend to the pinnacle of the real estate realm, positioning ourselves as the premier service provider in the region. With a steadfast dedication to innovation, integrity, and unparalleled service, we are committed to surpassing expectations and setting new standards of excellence in the real estate landscape. Join us on our journey as we redefine the future of real estate in the UAE and beyond.
